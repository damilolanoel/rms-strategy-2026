The SME Opportunity

Why SMEs are the whitespace CMRG and others have largely ignored

Nigeria's MSME sector contributes approximately 50% of GDP and accounts for 86.3% of employment (NBS 2021). Yet fewer than 3% of SMEs have ever commissioned formal market research. The reason is simple: the cost model of traditional agencies makes research inaccessible to businesses below ₦500m in annual revenue. RMS is purpose-built to close this gap.

Key Financial Policies

💳 Payment Terms

Corporate: 40% mobilisation on PO, 40% on fieldwork completion, 20% on final report. NGO/Govt: 50/50 or milestone-aligned. SME: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Retainers: monthly in advance. No final report released until 100% payment.

📊 Cash & DSO Targets

DSO targets: Q1 ≤60 days → Q4 ≤35 days. Monthly AR aging review. CEO escalation after 60 days overdue. Maintain minimum 8-week payroll cash reserve.

🏷️ Pricing Floor Rules

No project below 35% gross margin. SME products deliver ≥55% margin. No discounts beyond 10% without CEO approval. Research Lead must sign off on scope risks.

🧾 VAT & Tax Compliance

All invoices include 7.5% VAT. Monthly VAT returns by 21st. WHT certificates collected for reconciliation. Quarterly CIT installments filed.
